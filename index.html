Stefanie Hansen is a developer seeking to create a beautiful and functional user experience.

I chose to study programming because it simultaneously presents intellectual challenges and allows for a tremendous amount of creativity. It is vitally important to me to continually improve upon my skillset and learn new technologies. I approach roadblocks as opportunities to expand both my breadth and depth of knowledge.

In my free time, I often like to break away from the computer screen and go rock climbing or hiking. I have studied many things and am especially interested in the sciences. Given my particular interest in biology and nutrition, I love spending time gardening and reaping the benefits throughout the season. In general, I simply like trying new things and having novel experiences and, accordingly, love to travel. My dream is to explore as much as possible, engage others with unique perspectives, and come away a more well-rounded and compassionate person.

I believe that life is what you make it, and I want to leave the world a richer, kinder, and more interesting place. I enjoy working with others and I strive to achieve my goals. I am excited to meet you and work with you to create something awesome and unique!
